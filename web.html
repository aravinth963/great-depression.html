Great Depression

The Great Depression was a severe worldwide economic depression between 1929 and 1939 that began after a major fall in stock prices in the united states. the economic contagion began around september 4th, 1929, and know worldwide on Black Tuesday, the stock market crash of october 29th , 1929.

Between 1929 and 1932, worldwide (GDP) fell by an estimated 15% by comparision ,worldwide GDP fell by less than 1% from 2008 to 2009 during the great recession. Some economies started to recover by the mid-1030s. However, in many countries the negative effects of the Great Depression lasted until the beginning of world war II.
